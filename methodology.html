Methodology

Data :: I used UNHCR exclusively as my data source, to maintain some consistency, but even then, aggregated totals and totals of disaggregated counts had some discrepancies.
States :: The four states that surround Syria were chosen: Turkey to the north, Iraq to the east. Jordan to the south, and Lebanon to the west.
Provinces :: All provinces in which data were provided by UNHCR were used; this was all districts in these four states in which there are registered Syrians.
Intervals :: I chose seven increments - two per year since 2012 (may and november) + YTD (march) for 2015
Data Gaps :: For several of the month/state combinations,** only totals were available. In order to fill in the blanks, I determined the proportions that each of the districts in that state contributed to the total during the flanking months, and averaged these percentages. I then used the results against the totals to determine extrapolated values per district.
