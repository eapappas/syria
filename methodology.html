Methodology Behind the Syria Map

Data :: I used UNHCR exclusively as my data source, to maintain some consistency, but even then, aggregated totals and totals of disaggregated counts had some discrepancies.
States :: The four states that surround Syria were chosen: Turkey to the north, Iraq to the east. Jordan to the south, and Lebanon to the west.
Provinces :: Provinces for which UNCHR made data on registered Syrians available were used.
Intervals :: I chose seven intervals - two per year since 2012 (May and November) + YTD (March) for 2015
Data Gaps :: For several of the month/state combinations,** only totals were available. In order to fill in the blanks, I determined the proportions that each of the districts in that state contributed to the total for the flanking months, and averaged these percentages. I then used the resulting percentages against the totals to extrapolate values per district.
**
Iraq: May 2012, November 2012, May 2013;
Jordan: May 2012, November 2012, May 2013;
Turkey: May 2012
